2 Position Complex Motion Generation
  1. The Slider-Crank Mechanisms is one of the simplest mechanisms
  2. It is a four bar linkage system, consisting of :
  4. i) A crank - for circular motion
  5. ii) A slider - for linear, to-and-fro, motion
  6. iii) A coupler, and
  7. iv) optionally, a ground.
  9. The module on the graph shows the basic arrangement of the mechanism
